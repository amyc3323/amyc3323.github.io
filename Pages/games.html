Pizzapocalypse (2023)

Role: Producer, artist, animator
Tools: Unity, Aesprite, Autodesk Maya

What is the most logical course of action to take in the middle of a zombie apocalypse? Well obviously, you order a fresh hot pizza! In Pizzapocalypse, you play as a pizza delivery driver, swerving around zombies and crashing into obstacles to quickly deliver pizzas to your eagerly awaiting customers. The zombies? Well of course, they're trying to steal your delicious pizza! Deliver pizzas to increase your restaurantâ€™s rating and collect ingredients to cook even more delicious pizzas.

This game was made in 3 weeks during the National High School Game Academy program in 2023. Our final presentation presenting Pizzapocalypse can be watched on the left. It's also displayed on the official CMU NHSGA website! I worked with Dianne Cao, Ethan Kellogg, Ethan Bai, and Henry Shen as the producer and one of two artists. My main art contributions were the pizza delivery driver (all 16 directions of him!), the street tilemap, and all animations, but as the producer, I also scheduled all group meetings, planned the timeline, assigned tasks, and managed the team.

After the program, we went on to publish the game to the Apple appstore, so please check out Pizzapocalypse! on the App store.
