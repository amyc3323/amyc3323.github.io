Amy Chen

My name is Amy Chen, and I am currently an undergraduate studying computer science at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm also an artist with interests in theoretical cs, animation, game design, and UI/UX.

This website is an ongoing project of mine to learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However, it also serves as a place for me to display my work and act as a portfolio. Thus, everything you see here was drawn, written, or coded by me!
